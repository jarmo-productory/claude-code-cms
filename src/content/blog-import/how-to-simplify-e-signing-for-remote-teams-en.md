By leveraging Smart-ID, Agrello makes it possible to authenticate signatories easily and securely, reducing the risk of fraudulent signatures and ensuring that the integrity of each contract is upheld.

Additionally, Agrello’s integrations help bridge the gap between different parts of the business. This cross-functional efficiency is crucial in a remote setup, where physical handovers and in-person follow-ups aren’t possible. Agrello’s integrations ensure that every stakeholder, from sales to legal to operations, stays informed and aligned without unnecessary manual intervention.

The ability to integrate with tools like [Zapier](https://www.agrello.io/pages/zapier) also means that businesses can customize their workflows according to their unique needs.
