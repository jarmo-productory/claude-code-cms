## Welcome to Agrello

Getting started with Agrello is quick and easy. Follow these steps to set up your account and start signing documents.

### Step 1: Create Your Account

Visit [docs.agrello.io](https://docs.agrello.io/app/login?open_tab=sign_up) and sign up with your email address or use Smart-ID/Mobile-ID for instant verification.

### Step 2: Set Up Your Profile

Complete your profile by adding your name and company details. This information will appear on your signed documents.

### Step 3: Upload Your First Document

Click "New Document" and upload a PDF or Word document. Agrello will automatically prepare it for signing.

### Step 4: Invite Signers

Add the email addresses of people who need to sign. They'll receive an invitation with instructions.

### Need Help?

Contact our support team at support@agrello.io or book a [free workshop](https://calendly.com/agrello) with our team.
