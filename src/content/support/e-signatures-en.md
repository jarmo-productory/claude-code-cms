## Understanding E-Signatures

Electronic signatures are legally binding in the EU under the eIDAS regulation. Agrello supports multiple signature types to meet your needs.

### Signature Types

**Qualified Electronic Signatures (QES)**
The highest level of e-signature, equivalent to a handwritten signature. Available via Smart-ID, Mobile-ID, and national eID cards.

**Advanced Electronic Signatures (AdES)**
Uniquely linked to the signatory and capable of identifying them. Provides strong legal standing.

**Simple Electronic Signatures**
Basic e-signatures suitable for internal documents and lower-risk agreements.

### Supported Authentication Methods

- **Smart-ID** - Mobile app-based authentication
- **Mobile-ID** - SIM-based authentication
- **ID Cards** - National eID card readers
- **Email verification** - For simple signatures

### Legal Validity

All signatures created through Agrello comply with EU eIDAS regulations and are legally valid across all EU member states.
