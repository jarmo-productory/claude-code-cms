- **Top keywords:** "käsundusleping" (590 clicks), "litsentsileping" (179), "tööleping" variations
- **Recommendation:** Migrate ALL Estonian legal content - it's your competitive moat

### Latvian (LV) - EXPAND
- **Best CTR in any market:** 10.98%
- **Current content is limited** but performs well
- **Top keywords:** "darba līguma paraugs 2025" (20 clicks, position 3.24)
- **Recommendation:** Create more Latvian versions of successful Estonian content

### English (EN) - SELECTIVE
- **ChatGPT/AI content performs well** (1,151 clicks on ChatGPT legal drafting)
- **Technical content (ASIC, PAdES)** ranks well
- **Generic SOP/paperless content** has impressions but poor positions
- **Recommendation:** Focus on AI + legal + technical niche. Don't compete on generic terms.
